3. Navigating Search Algorithms: A Tool Kit

Submitted by Erin Brock Carlson

This toolkit offers a breakdown of how to more ethically engage with the search function on popular platforms including Amazon, Google, Instagram, Pinterest, Snapchat, TikTok, Twitter, and YouTube. Each page in the toolkit breaks down the functionality of searching on the specific platform. We offer practical analysis of what ethical conundrums, paradoxes, and problems that exist in the results various searches on these platforms yield, and in the ways users are urged to engage with or to avoid the platformâ€™s search function. The toolkit also includes Future Work and Further Reading pages which puts our work into conversation with others who are exploring parallel questions.

1. Data Brokers: Last Week Tonight with John Oliver

Submitted by Dr. Gavin Johnson.

In this video from Last Week Tonight with John Oliver, the host discusses the knowledge that data brokers know about average users and how they use our personal information for their own benefit. Oliver also suggests a unique way to changing privacy laws in the United States.

2.Privacy + Surveillance & Course Hero Podcast

Submitted by Dr. Charles Woods

In this podcast, DRPC Advisory Board member Dr. Charles Woods chats with Dr. Lacy Hope from Utah Tech University and Dr. Brenna Clarke Gray from Thompson Rivers University about issues related to privacy and surveillance on Course Hero. According to their website, Course Hero is an online learning platform for course-specific study resources that supports students on their unique learning journey as they seek help and resources to better understand their coursework, prepare for exams, learn and remember.
