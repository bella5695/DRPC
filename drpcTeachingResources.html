Assignments and Activities

Purpose of Assignments and Activities

This section includes activities and assignments which can be used in various writing activities adapted for any classroom.

This section will evolve and expand as the DRP Collective welcomes new members.

Please visit our Contact page if you would like to submit an assignment or activity related to digital privacy and surveillance to the DRP Collective

On this page, you can find the activites and assignments developed by our team.

On the right, is the viewable preview or link of the activity and assignment, while on the left is a description of the activity and assignment.

Description of Assignments and Activities:

1. The Rhetoric of Secrecy & Surveillance Untextbook by Atilla Hallsby

This website covers three genres:

  • secrecy and surveillance history
  • secrecy and surveillance theories
  • wicked problems that are related to secrecy and surveillence

    • The Rhetoric of Secrecy & Surveillance Untextbook by Atilla Hallsby: Link to Website

    2. Negotiating Digital Footprints by Morgan Banville

    This activity is about a team at the office is using Microsoft Teams. However, the messages are being seen to everyone who has access to the channel in the company. It is important for the organization to maintain a level of surveillance and privacy within the workplace. The assignments for this case is intended for others to explore best practices of commmunication and the impacts of surveillance and privacy within the professional workplace.

    Negotiating Digital Footprints by Morgan Banville: View in PDF

    3. Privacy Policy Remediation Assignment by Charles Woods

    The assignment is about developing a sense of privacy and impacts of Big Data on ourselves as well as the society.

    Privacy Policy Remediation Assignment by Charles Woods: View in PDF

    4. Basic Principles of Algorithms by Noah Wason

    This assignment is about encouraging participation. There are different activities for the participants to get engaged in.

    Basic Principles of Algorithms by Noah Wason: View in PDF

    5. Big Data Journal Activity by Charles Woods

    The purpose of this activity is for researching and collecting articles about Big Data, so that the researchers can read and gain a unique perspective regarding Big Data.

    Big Data Journal Activity by Charles Woods: View in PDF
