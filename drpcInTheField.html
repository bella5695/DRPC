DRPC In the Field

This is where you can find the list of awards, grants, and presentations showing DRPC in action in the field!

Awards and Grants

  • NCTE/CCCC Emergent Research Grant, (2022-2023). This award is funded up to $10 000 each for proposals by the CCCC. The principal investigator must belong to CCCC at the time of submission of hte proposal. The proposed projects are expected to last to two years, but can run for shorter periods of time.

  • CPTSC Research Grant. DRPC Presidential GAR (grant-funded research assistant) was funded $1950 to the DRPC to further examine the design and transfer of technical communication skills related to digital privacy and surveillance in technical and scientific communication programs in Texas.

  • TAMUC Presidential GAR Initiative: Funded. DRPC was awarded $16666 as this competitive award creates the opportunity for a graduate student researcher to work with the Digital Rhetorical Privacy Collective (drpcollective.com) to gain practical experience in grant writing, cultivating community partnerships, resource development and production, and digital presence management.
